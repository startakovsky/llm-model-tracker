## Architecture

Mistral Small 4 is the next major release in the Mistral Small family, unifying the capabilities of several flagship Mistral models into a single system:

- Combines strong reasoning from Mistral Medium with the efficiency of the Small line
- Multimodal: accepts text and image inputs, outputs text
- 262K token context window
- Apache 2.0 licensed — fully open weights
- Optimized for instruction following, function calling, and enterprise deployments

## Quality Assessment

Mistral Small 4 represents Mistral's consolidation play — bringing flagship-tier capabilities into a lightweight, efficient package. At $0.15/$0.60 per million tokens, it is one of the cheapest multimodal open models available, competitive with Qwen3.5-9B ($0.10/$0.15) on price while offering a larger context window and image understanding.

**Cost/quality framing:** Roughly 10× cheaper than GLM-5.2 ($0.94/$2.95) while delivering perhaps 70% of its capability on standard tasks. Ideal for high-volume production workloads, RAG pipelines, and cost-sensitive agentic applications.

**Verdict:** A solid lightweight open model for budget-conscious deployments. Not a frontier contender, but excellent value per dollar for production text+image workloads.
